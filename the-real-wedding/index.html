The auspicious times for important rituals

5 November 2024

Engagement & Lagnapatrika

engagement, or Nischayathartham, formalizes the alliance with rituals and the exchange of rings. The Lagnapatrika, detailing wedding details, is exchanged between families. The event is a joyful celebration of unity and blessings.

13 November 2024

Pasupu Dhanchadam

Pasupu Dhanchadam involves exchanging turmeric between families, symbolizing purity and auspiciousness. This ritual strengthens the bond and blesses the couple for a harmonious union.

6 December 2024

Pandhiri Rata

Pandhiri Rata is a festive pre-wedding ritual in Telugu culture where vibrant decorations and canopies are set up at the bride’s and groom’s homes. It signifies the official start of wedding celebrations with joyous music and community participation.

14 December 2024

Pellikuthuru

Pellikuthuru event is a pre-wedding ritual in Telugu culture where the bride is adorned with turmeric and other auspicious items, symbolizing purification and beauty. It marks the bride's transition to married life with joyous celebrations and family blessings.
